The Youniverse Explorer

3 August 2021

The Youniverse Explorer model is now back on sale.

This edition of the model comes fully assembled. Originally designed by Douglas Harding in the 1970s, this is a new model of our place in the universe. It is important in terms of a new view of who we are in the same way as the first photograph of the Earth from the Moon was important and inspiring, or the first model of the solar system. This model, however, includes every layer or ourselves, including the Centre. Not only is this a work of genius, bringing together every level of our body and mind into one model, but it is also beautiful, a work of art. We have about 200 hundred of these amazing models for sale. (They stand about 35 cm tall.) This is likely to be the last time we produce this model. Certainly a collector's item!
